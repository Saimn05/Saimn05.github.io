Gravity-Powered Car (Recycled Materials)

Design notes

  • Body: lightweight styrofoam or recycled plastic shaped for low drag.
  • Wheels: low friction bearings and aligned axles; holes placed as in sketches.
  • Ballast: added metal block in a cavity (see sketch 2) to tune mass distribution for downhill runs.
  • Testing: adjust axle alignment and wheel symmetry to minimize frictional losses.
