Gravity-Powered Car (Recycled Materials)

Overview

Testbed for evaluating how mass placement and axle alignment impact downhill performance.

Materials & fabrication

  • Body: recycled wood / foam variants in concept stage.
  • Wheels: simple discs; future upgrade to low-friction bearings.
  • Ballast: adjustable metal mass inside cavity.

Measurements & results

Ballast shifts of only millimeters produced measurable differences in run distance. Alignment errors increased drag via lateral oscillation.
