Gravity-Powered Car (Recycled Materials)

Overview

Testbed for evaluating how mass placement and axle alignment impact downhill performance.

Materials & fabrication

  • Body: recycled wood / foam variants.
  • Wheels: discs; future bearing upgrade.
  • Ballast: adjustable internal mass.

Measurements & results

Millimeter-scale ballast shifts changed distance measurably; misalignment introduced lateral drag.
