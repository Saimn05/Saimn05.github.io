Flight Path Simulation (Python)

Model & assumptions

Simplified point‑mass aircraft; empirical drag; thrust and fuel model; linear lift in operating AoA band.

Numerical method

RK4 fixed‑step integrator; PID trials for altitude/heading hold under changing wind vectors.

How to run

Dependencies: numpy, matplotlib. Example: python3 simulate.py --altitude 34000 --wind -20.
