Flight Path Simulation (Python)

Model & assumptions

Simplified point-mass aircraft with empirical drag coefficient, thrust/fuel model; lift approximated linearly around the operating AoA.

Numerical method

RK4 fixed-step integrator; simple PID for heading/altitude hold experiments.

How to run

Dependencies: numpy, matplotlib. Example: python3 simulate.py --altitude 34000 --wind -20. Output saved to plots/.
