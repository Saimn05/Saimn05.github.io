Flight Path Simulation (Python)

Model & assumptions

Simplified point-mass aircraft, empirical drag coefficient, thrust and fuel-burn model. Lift approximated linearly around the operating angle-of-attack range.

Numerical method

RK4 fixed-step integrator (dt chosen for stability). Implemented a PID controller for altitude/heading hold experiments; plotted fuel flow against altitude for different wind cases.

Results

Plots show that headwinds increase fuel flow at cruise more than tailwinds reduce it — highlights the importance of wind-aware flight planning for fuel efficiency.

How to run

Dependencies: numpy, matplotlib. Example: python3 simulate.py --altitude 34000 --wind -20. Output images saved to plots/.
